# Privacy Policy for CleverDict

**Last Updated: March 29, 2026**

## Overview

CleverDict is an AI-powered English dictionary application for macOS. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

CleverDict does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected (name, email, location, etc.)
- **No usage analytics** or tracking is performed
- **No cookies** or similar technologies are used
- **No data is shared** with third parties

## Network Usage

CleverDict connects to AI language model APIs solely to generate dictionary definitions for words you look up. The words you search are sent to the API provider to generate responses. No search history is stored on any server.

## Local Data

All application preferences and settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted anywhere.

## Third-Party Services

CleverDict uses third-party AI APIs to provide dictionary definitions. These services have their own privacy policies. No personally identifiable information is sent to these services — only the words you choose to look up.

## Children's Privacy

CleverDict does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page.
