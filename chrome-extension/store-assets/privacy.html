Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device. Always.

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Lexis is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your vocabulary learning data is personal and should stay on your device. This policy explains exactly what data Lexis collects and how it handles your information.

Key principle: All vocabulary and settings data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers unless you explicitly use our cloud sync feature.

What Data We Collect

Lexis processes text you select to provide dictionary definitions. Here's how your data is handled:

Data Type Storage Transmission
Vocabulary words
Words you mark for learning		 Your device only Never sent
Settings & preferences
Interface language, API settings		 Your device only Never sent
Selected text
Words you look up		 Not stored Sent to your AI provider
Vocabulary (with sync)
Optional cloud backup		 Your account Encrypted sync only

How Your Data Is Used

AI Definitions

When you look up a word, the selected text is sent to your configured AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, or compatible API). This is necessary to generate definitions. We do not receive, store, or have access to this data.

Your AI API key is stored locally on your device and is only used to authenticate with your chosen provider.

Vocabulary Notebook

Words you mark are stored in Chrome's local storage (chrome.storage.local). This data:

  • Stays on your device unless you enable cloud sync
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • Can be exported or deleted at any time

Optional Cloud Sync

If you enable the cloud sync feature:

  • Your vocabulary is encrypted before transmission
  • Data is synced only between your own devices
  • You can disable sync and delete synced data anytime

Permissions We Request

  • storage — To save your vocabulary and settings locally
  • activeTab — To enable lookups only on the tab you're using
  • alarms — To periodically sync your vocabulary (when sync is enabled)
  • <all_urls> — To detect text selection on any webpage

Third-Party Services

Lexis uses third-party AI services to generate definitions:

  • OpenAI (api.openai.com)
  • Anthropic (api.anthropic.com)
  • Or any OpenAI-compatible API you configure

When you use these services, their respective privacy policies apply. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of your chosen AI provider.

Children's Privacy

Lexis is not specifically designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used our extension, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Export — Download your vocabulary as a text file anytime
  • Delete — Remove individual words or clear your entire vocabulary
  • Disable — Uninstall the extension to remove all local data
  • Control — Your API key and settings can be changed or removed in Settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
